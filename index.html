The 12 data requests shown in this demo (R1–R12)
R1challenges_assigned — who got which challenges each cycle (the completion KPI's denominator)
R2claim_source on claims — manual / auto_expiry / summary_forced (engagement vs system actions)
R3challenge_config_id — stable identity; step recycles when the list loops
R4Event_ended additions — end reason + per-player totals
R5challenge_unlocked — background progress waiting at unlock
R6bar_shown — one impression per session (bar CTR, red-dot effect)
R7progress checkpoints at 25/50/75% (where players give up)
R8completion context — time, qualifying bets, distinct games
R9Play destination — which game the player was routed to
R10challenge_substituted — risk exclusions swap the target, progress retained
R11popup open context + view duration (funnel + fatigue)
R12challenge_expired_progress — the near-miss record at cycle end
Presenter notes — a 5-step demo script
  1. Load the page: R1 (assignment) and R6 (impression) are already in the log before the player does anything. "The most important record happens before the first click."
  2. Open the popup, press Play, spin with bet 0.05: winnings land, but no challenge progress (min bet rule). Switch to 0.10 and watch R7 checkpoints.
  3. Complete and claim: R2 = manual; the next challenge unlocks with background progress (R5), sometimes straight to Claim.
  4. Press "End cycle" three times: on day 4 the list loops — same challenge ids, new positions (R3's reason). Unclaimed rewards auto-collect (R2 = auto_expiry) and unfinished challenges write R12.
  5. Press "Risk-exclude Jini Studios" (R10) — and press it again for total exclusion (Game Unavailable). Then "End event" and read R4's closing totals. Blue lines = the spec today; amber lines = the gap we're asking to close.
